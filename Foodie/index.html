Hyderabadi Biryani

One of the most popular biryani recipe, Hyderabadi Biryani is flavourful and delectable. Here, half boiled rice is layered with fried onions, mint, cooked mutton, sealed with dough and slow cooked 'dum' style.

Lucknowi Biryani

Lucknowi biryani or Awadhi biryani is a aromatic dish made with a combination of meat, spices and flavourful rice. Sometimes there is use of nuts, saffron and milk too.

Kolkata Biryani

Made with aromatic rice, tender meat, a piece of boiled potato and egg, the Kolkata Biryani has long been delighting the palates of the City of Joy since over a century now.

Ambur Biryani

Originating from Tamil Nadu, this biryani was made famous by the cooks of the Arcot Nawabs. Hasin Baig, a royal cook,the distinct aroma of seeraga samba rice mixed with spicy gravy and tender meat gives the dish an irresistible taste, making it a must-try dish.

Malabar Biryani

Here, the authentic biryani recipe gets a regional spin and replaces succulent meat chunks with soft and tender fish fillet. Malabari fish biryani tastes the best when paired with raita.

About Biryani Blues

At Biryani Blues, we believe that biryani is more than just a dish; it’s a celebration of flavor, tradition, and culture. Nestled in the heart of Kolkata, our restaurant is dedicated to bringing you the finest and most authentic biryanis from across India.

Our recipes are crafted with love and care, using traditional methods passed down through generations. Each biryani is a harmonious blend of aromatic spices, high-quality ingredients, and slow-cooked perfection.
